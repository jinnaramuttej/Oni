# Premium Color Palettes

A curated guide to luxury and modern color systems suited for different business verticals. These palettes avoid default, saturated web colors in favor of balanced HSL tones and deep dark layers.

---

## 1. Tech & SaaS
A sleek, futuristic color scheme optimized for software products, digital platforms, and tech agencies.

*   **Dark Background (`--bg`)**: `#0F0F1A` (Deep navy midnight)
*   **Primary Accent (`--p`)**: `#7C3AED` (Royal violet)
*   **Secondary Accent (`--s`)**: `#06B6D4` (Electric cyan)
*   **Light Background (`--light`)**: `#F8F8FF` (Off-white ice)
*   **Text color (`--text`)**: `#FFFFFF`
*   **Muted Text (`--text-muted`)**: `rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.6)`
*   **Visual Characteristic**: Glowing cyan/purple abstract backgrounds, semi-transparent dark container overlays.
