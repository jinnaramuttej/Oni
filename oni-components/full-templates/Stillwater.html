01 — Our philosophy

Practice, not performance.

We opened Stillwater with a single belief: that the hour you spend on a mat should feel like the opposite of the rest of your day. No mirrors to correct yourself in, no leaderboards, no heat turned up to impress. Just cedar underfoot, breath in your chest, and a room full of people quietly doing the same honest work.

  • Small by design

    Twelve mats, never more. A teacher who knows your name, your knees, and the story your shoulders are telling today.

  • Rooted in nature

    Cedar floors, linen curtains, a courtyard of ferns. We practise with the windows open to the seasons, not sealed against them.

  • Kindness to beginners

    There is no level you must reach to belong here. Rest is participation. Leaving early is fine. You already qualify.

“Teach this triple truth to all: a generous heart, kind speech, and a life of service are the things which renew humanity.”

Words on our studio wall

03 — Meditation & breathwork

Stillness is a skill — we teach it.

Meditation and breath are not the absence of effort; they are a craft, refined slowly in good company. Our programs are secular, evidence-informed, and designed for real, busy, modern minds.

  • I
    Meditation course

    SIT — Mindfulness Foundations

    A six-week course in seated practice: posture, attention, and kindness toward a wandering mind. Daily ten-minute home sits, supported by Sunday circles.

    6 weeks · 90 min·Sundays, 5:00 pm

  • II
    Guided meditation

    Morning Silence

    Twenty minutes of guided arrival, twenty of shared silence, tea afterwards in the courtyard. The quietest way to start a weekday.

    45 min·Mon / Wed / Fri, 7:30 am

  • III
    Breathwork

    Coherent Breath

    Five to six breaths per minute, guided with tone and subtle pacing. Grounding, measurable, and deeply calming for anxiety and restless sleep.

    45 min·Tuesdays, 6:00 pm

  • IV
    Guided rest

    Yoga Nidra — The Rest Practice

    Lie down, cover your eyes, and be led to the threshold of sleep. One session restores like hours of rest — no effort required.

    60 min·Thursdays, 7:30 pm

  • V
    Advanced breathwork

    Breath & Ice

    For experienced practitioners: an energising breath sequence followed by a supervised cold plunge in our courtyard. Intense, clear, alive.

    90 min·Saturdays, 9:00 am

All meditation sits are free for members. First-time visitors are welcome to join any open sit as our guest — simply arrive, take a cushion, and close your eyes.
